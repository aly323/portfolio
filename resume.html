Hello everyone! My name is Amy and I'm interested at the intersection between psychology and neuroscience. As a motivated, creative, and resourceful individual, I've developed a work ethic that allows me to be both effective independently and collaboratively with other peers.

I've worked in psychology and neurology laboratories, from the research assistant position, within Massachusetts General Hospital and Tufts University. I've also been working as a Teaching Assistant for the Computer Science Department at Tufts.

At the end of the day, I want to use psychology and computer science in the context of human factors engineering. To work towards a better future. Whether it's investigating into our inner psychology, designing accessible solutions for everyday problems, or creating a new technology, I want to make living life a little easier.
